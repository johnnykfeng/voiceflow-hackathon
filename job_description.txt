DATA ANALYST - IT


THE JOB
Reporting to the CIO, the Data Analyst will be responsible for interpreting complex data sets, analyzing trends, and providing actionable insights to support business decisions. The ideal candidate will have a strong analytical mindset and be able to work collaboratively with cross-functional teams.

HOURS OF WORK: Monday to Friday, 8:00AM – 5:00PM


DUTIES & RESPONSIBILITIES

Gather and analyze large data sets to identify patterns, trends, and insights
Develop and maintain dashboards, reports, and metrics to track key performance indicators (KPIs)
Collaborate with cross-functional teams to identify business problems and develop data-driven solutions
Work with business stakeholders to understand data requirements and design data models to ensure data accuracy and consistency
Design and implement data quality and governance processes
Develop and maintain data pipelines using Microsoft Azure technologies, including Azure Data Factory, Azure Data Lake, and Azure Databricks
Conduct ad-hoc analysis to support business initiatives and answer complex questions
Communicate findings and insights to stakeholders through clear and compelling data visualizations and presentations
Continuously monitor and evaluate data quality to ensure data integrity
Stay up-to-date with industry trends and best practices to identify new opportunities for analysis and optimization

KNOWLEDGE & SKILLS REQUIRED

Bachelor's degree in Mathematics, Statistics, Computer Science, or related field
2+ years of experience in data analysis or related field
Strong analytical skills with experience using statistical analysis tools such as Python, R, or SAS
Strong SQL skills and experience with database design and development
Experience with Azure Data Factory, Azure Data Lake, and Azure Databricks
Experience in data visualization and reporting tools such as Power BI or Tableau
Strong communication skills and ability to collaborate with cross-functional teams
Ability to manage multiple projects and priorities in a fast-paced environment
Detail-oriented with strong problem-solving skills
Experience with machine learning and predictive analytics
Knowledge of data warehousing and ETL processes
